### Offices and Databases

- European Patent Office (EPO) database [Espacenet.com](https://worldwide.espacenet.com/)
- US Patent and Trademark Office (uspto) database Patent Public Search [Basic (PPUBS Basic)](https://ppubs.uspto.gov/basic/) or [Advanced](https://ppubs.uspto.gov/pubwebapp/)
- World Intellectual Property Organization (WIPO) Database [PATENTSCOPE](https://patentscope.wipo.int)
- [Google Patents](https://patents.google.com)

### Search Methodology

- Search all patents assigned to LEGO, specifically, the _LEGO A/S_. Each database requires a specific query:
  - Patentscope: **PA:"LEGO A/S"**
  - Google Patents: **assignee:(Lego A/S)**
- Sort by _Publication Date_
